COYO LAM - ABOUT ME

about me

  Hi there! (＾◡＾)

  My name is Coyo and I am a full-fledged artist born and raised in Dallas, Texas. I am also a software developer!


  I started my interest in web design at the age of nine when I discovered that code could manipulate websites in a way that allowed my creativity to merge with functionality.

  As I got older, I never let go of my playfulness with graphic design and have used my experience with tools like Photoshop and Illustrator to design product packaging, labels, and website layouts.

  I love the internet because it serves as the ultimate platform. What I love about programming is having the power to utilize this platform for my voice. I'm inspired by blocks of color and the natural formations of the planet. Although this website I've created features the nostalgic retro-style of the early digital era, my true calling is in using my voice to protect our Earth.

  Another project I am working on is my resource Food for Gardens. I want to use this platform to share all of the edible plants that exist around us, and encourage others to turn their clean cut lawns into bio-available food forests!

 When I'm not spending hours in front of the computer editing code, I'm painting, hiking with my dogs, snack-hunting, and baking bread. I love making new friends, so don't be a stranger!

Thanks for stopping by!
~ Coyo
